# KnowledgeBase

## Login troubleshooting

When a customer says they cannot log in:

- Confirm whether the issue happens in a normal browser window and in an incognito/private window.
- Ask the customer to clear cookies and site data for the application domain.
- Confirm whether the customer reaches the multi-factor authentication screen.
- If the customer recently reset their password, ask them to close all existing browser tabs and start a new session.
- If the customer is locked out after multiple failed attempts, escalate to Identity Support.

## Password reset guidance

If a password reset link does not work:

- Ask the customer to request a fresh password reset email.
- Confirm the customer is using the newest reset email, since older reset links can expire.
- Ask the customer to check spam, quarantine, or email security tools if the reset email is missing.
- If the email address is managed by SSO, direct the customer to sign in through their identity provider.

## Billing duplicate charge guidance

When a customer reports a duplicate charge:

- Check whether one charge is a posted charge and the other is a pending authorization.
- Pending authorizations usually disappear automatically within 1 to 3 business days.
- If both charges post, collect the invoice number and escalate to Billing Operations.
- Avoid promising an immediate refund until the payment status is confirmed.

## Data sync guidance

When a customer reports delayed sync:

- Check the connector status and the most recent successful sync timestamp.
- Confirm whether the customer changed credentials or permissions recently.
- Ask the customer not to repeatedly disconnect and reconnect unless instructed, because that can delay queued backfills.
- If the backfill job has completed, ask the customer to refresh the workspace and verify the records.
