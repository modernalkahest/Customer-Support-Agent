# Customer Service Policy

## Food Delivery Platform

### 1. Purpose

This Customer Service Policy defines the standards, commitments, and procedures governing customer support interactions across the platform. The goal is to ensure consistent, fair, and timely resolution of customer concerns while maintaining operational efficiency.

### 2. Scope

This policy applies to:

All customers using the platform
All orders placed through the mobile app or website
All support channels (chat, email, call)

### 3. Service Commitment
We are committed to:

Providing timely and accurate support
Ensuring fair resolutions based on issue type
Maintaining transparency in communication
Delivering a customer-first experience within operational constraints

### 4. Support Channels & Availability
| Channel	| Availability	| Typical Response Time |
|---------------| -----------------|-------------|
| In-app Chat	| 24/7	| Within minutes |
| Email |	24/7 |	Within 24 hours |
| Phone (if applicable) |	Business hours |	Immediate |

### 5. Order Issue Resolution Policy

#### 5.1 Non-Delivery of Order
If an order is marked as delivered but not received:

The issue will be investigated using delivery records
If confirmed or reasonably suspected:
Full refund or redelivery will be provided

| Delay Beyond ETD Upper Limit     | Action                         |
| -------------------------------- | ------------------------------ |
| 0–10 minutes                     | No compensation                |
| 10–20 minutes                    | Apology + small coupon         |
| 20–30 minutes                    | Partial refund (10–25%)        |
| 30–45 minutes                    | Higher partial refund (25–50%) |
| >45 minutes OR order compromised | Full refund                    |

#### 5.2 Delayed Delivery
If delivery exceeds the estimated time:
Minor delays → goodwill compensation (e.g., coupons)
Significant delays → partial refund may be issued

#### 5.3 Incorrect or Missing Items
Refund will be issued for missing or incorrect items
In select cases, replacement may be arranged

#### 5.4 Food Quality Issues

For concerns such as:
Spoilage
Spillage
Unacceptable quality

We may provide:

Partial or full refund based on severity

#### 5.5 Order Cancellation
Before restaurant confirmation → Full refund
After preparation begins → Cancellation fees may apply

#### 5.6 Payment Issues

In cases of:

Failed transactions with deducted amounts
Duplicate charges

Refunds will be processed within 3–7 business days, depending on the payment provider.

### 6. Refund Policy
Refunds are processed to the original payment method unless stated otherwise
Processing time depends on banks/payment gateways
Promotional credits may be offered in lieu of refunds in certain cases

### 7. Customer Responsibilities

Customers are expected to:

Provide accurate delivery address and contact details
Be available to receive orders
Raise issues within a reasonable timeframe (typically within 24 hours)
Use the platform in good faith

### 8. Delivery Limitations

We do not guarantee:

Exact delivery times (only estimates)
Food quality beyond restaurant preparation standards
Availability of all items at all times

Delays may occur due to:

Weather conditions
Traffic disruptions
Restaurant preparation delays
High demand periods

### 9. Abuse & Misuse Policy

We reserve the right to:

Limit or deny compensation in cases of repeated misuse
Suspend or restrict accounts engaging in fraudulent behavior
Investigate suspicious activity

### 10. Escalation Policy

If a customer is not satisfied:

Issues may be escalated internally for review
Final resolution will be communicated within a reasonable timeframe

### 11. Data & Privacy

All customer interactions are handled in accordance with applicable data protection regulations. Personal data is used only for service delivery and support purposes.

### 12. Policy Updates

This policy may be updated periodically to reflect:

Operational changes
Regulatory requirements
Service improvements

Customers will be notified of significant updates where required.
